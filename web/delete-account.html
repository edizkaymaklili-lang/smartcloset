Smart Closet – Account Deletion Request

You can request the deletion of your Smart Closet account and all associated data by following the steps below. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent. All your wardrobe items, style posts, and personal data will be permanently removed.

What data will be deleted

  1. Your profile (name, email, profile photo)
  2. All wardrobe items and clothing photos
  3. All Style Feed posts and comments
  4. Notifications and activity history
  5. All account preferences and settings

How to delete your account

Option 1 – From the app:

  1. Open Smart Closet
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Option 2 – By email:

Send an email to support@smartcloset.app with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 days.
